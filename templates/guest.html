PidginForge is a collaborative foreign language learning community. We promote peace and prosperity through greater understanding of other cultures.

We are currently on version 1.0 (deployed March 2021), with many future functions not yet enabled. Current features include: Question/Answer Forum; Vocabulary Stacks with limited exercises; Audio/Video/Text learning media with the ability to attach learner notes; and an Events Calendar to facilitate gatherings.

Please be patient in these early phases as we work to build a robust and reliable platform.

For the safety of our users, you must register in order to see the good stuff.
